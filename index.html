About us

Welcome to iTech'Demy

iTechDemy is an online learning platform that offers technology courses for people who want to improve their skills, from coding to no-code. The platform was founded by iTechTeam, a technology company that helps its clients establish and grow digital businesses with innovative solutions. iTechTeam believes in sharing their knowledge and expertise with those who want to join the tech world, overcome poverty and unemployment, and improve their lives.

iTechDemy provides affordable and accessible tech courses to learners in Nigeria and other African countries, particularly those in rural areas. The platform aims to make learning in the technology and education sectors easy and smart.
